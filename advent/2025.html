FPIndia Advent 2025!

FPIndia Advent Calendar January 2025 is a month long event to share functional programming stories, experiences, and resources by the FPIndia community. The event is inspired by the F# Advent Calendar.

The rules are -

  • Choose a Functional Programming related topic for your blog post or youtube video and reserve the slot by messaging on Telegram, or Twitter. Please note that you do not have to announce the topic until the date (but you can).
  • Open a PR on the repository for this website to add your slot to the table below.
  • OR Wait for someone from the FPIndia community to accept your slot and add it to the table below.
  • Prepare a blog post or video in English or any regional Indian language.
  • Publish your post on a specified date (according to the calendar).
  • Post the link to your post on Telegram or Twitter with hashtags #FPIndia and #FPIndiaAdvent.
